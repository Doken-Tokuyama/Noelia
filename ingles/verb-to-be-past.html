El Verbo TO BE en Pasado

El verbo TO BE en pasado tiene dos formas: WAS y WERE

Pronombre
Verbo TO BE
Ejemplo
I
was

You
were

He/She/It

We

They
were
They were friends

Truco para recordar: WAS para singular (I, he, she, it) y WERE para plural (you, we, they)

Preguntas

Para hacer preguntas, ponemos el verbo antes del sujeto:

Preguntas con WAS

Was + I/he/she/it + ...?
Was I late for the meeting? Was she your teacher? Was it difficult?

Preguntas con WERE

Were + you/we/they + ...?
Were you at the concert? Were we invited? Were they happy?

Respuestas Cortas

Positivas
Yes, I was. Yes, you were. Yes, he/she/it was. Yes, we/they were.
Negativas
No, I wasn't. No, you weren't. No, he/she/it wasn't. No, we/they weren't.
